Where the data come from?


A more or less detailed list of geoportals providing the elevation or the point cloud data used for creating the Shaded Maps.


Australia

The Elvis - Elevation and Depth - Foundation Spatial Data geoportal by GeoScience Australia is the main resource for the point cloud data, in particular for the cities of Sydney, Canberra, Adelaide, Brisbane, Cairns and Hobart.

Point cloud data for the city of Melbourne are available in DataVic, the Open Data Platform for the State of Victoria.
